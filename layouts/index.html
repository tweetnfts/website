FAQ

Is every interaction with my tweet reflected in the NFT?

Yes! Metrics like likes, comments, impressions, retweets, quote retweets, and bookmarks are all tracked and regularly updated in the NFT's traits.

Are there any fees?

If you mint tweets one by one, the cost is $10 per tweet. However, you can take our Twitterati Mega Pack priced at $100 to mint up to 20 tweets at once, offering you a 50% price reduction per tweet.

Any future functionalities you are working on?

We are incredibly for the future of TweetNFTs, and have a ton of new features coming up. These include the launch of a browser extension, a Twitter bot, web2 payment gateways, as well as many new improvements.
