FAQ

Can a tweet be minted multiple times as an NFT?

Yes! One same tweet can be minted multiple times as an NFT. This is an example of the beauty of ERC-1155, which enables both fungible and non-fungible tokens to coexist.

Are there any fees?

If you mint tweets one by one, the cost is $10 per tweet. However, you can take our Twitterati Mega Pack priced at $100 to mint up to 20 tweets at once, offering you a 50% price reduction per tweet.

Any future functionalities you are working on?

We are incredibly excited for the future of TweetNFTs, and have a ton of new features coming up. These include the launch of a browser extension, a Twitter bot, web2 payment gateways, as well as many new improvements.
